1.ldli

Ingredients


- 2 cups idli rice
- 1 cup urad dal
- 1/2 cup fenugreek seeds
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- Water (as needed)
- Oil or ghee for greasing

Step-by-Step Instructions

1. Rinse idli rice, urad dal, and fenugreek seeds separately. Soak idli rice and fenugreek seeds in water for 4-5 hours. Soak urad dal in water for 4-5 hours.
Grinding (30 minutes) 2. Drain and grind urad dal into a smooth paste. Drain and grind idli rice and fenugreek seeds together into a coarse paste. Mix both pastes together.
Fermentation (7-8 hours) 4.Transfer the mixture to a large vessel. Add salt and mix well. Cover and let ferment overnight (7-8 hours).
5.Idli Making (30 minutes) Grease idli molds with oil or ghee. Pour the fermented batter into molds. Steam idlis for 15-20 minutes.
6.Serving Serve idlis hot with sambar, chutney, or podi.

3.dosa

Ingredients

- 2 cups idli rice
2 cups idli rice
1 cup urad dal
1/2 cup fenugreek seeds
1/2 teaspoon salt
Water (as needed)
Oil or ghee for greasing

Day 1: Preparation 1. Soak rice, urad dal, and fenugreek seeds separately (4-5 hours). Grind into a smooth paste. Mix and ferment overnight (7-8 hours).
Day 2: Dosa Making1. Heat a non-stick pan or dosa tava. Pour a ladle of batter and spread evenly. Cook for 2 minutes (until bottom is golden).Flip and cook for 1 minute. Serve hot with sambar and chutney.

4.Arcot -makkan peda

Ingredients

- 1 cup grated khoya
- 1/2 cup grated paneer
- 1/4 cup powdered sugar
- 1/4 teaspoon cardamom powder
- 1/4 teaspoon saffron threads
- 1 tablespoon ghee
- Chopped pistachios

Preparation (10 minutes) 1. Mix khoya, paneer, sugar, cardamom powder, and saffron. Knead until smooth. Shaping (10 minutes) Divide mixture into small portions. Shape into pedas (flat or round shapes). Cooking (5 minutes) Heat ghee in a pan.Cook pedas until lightly browned.GarnishSprinkle chopped nuts (if using). Serve warm or at room temperature. Tips:- Use high-quality khoya and paneer. Adjust sugar to taste.Don't overcook pedas.Store in airtight container.
